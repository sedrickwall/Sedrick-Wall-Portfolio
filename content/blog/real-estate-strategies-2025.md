## **1. Mid-Term Rentals (30‚Äì180 Days) Continue to Outperform**  
Mid-term rentals (MTRs) are one of the most reliable investment models moving into 2025 because they combine:

- Higher income than long-term rentals  
- More predictability than short-term rentals  
- A steady demand pool  

### **Who books mid-term rentals:**
- Traveling nurses  
- Corporate relocations  
- Tech contractors  
- Remote workers  
- Families in transition  

### **How to win with MTRs in 2025:**
- Choose locations near **hospitals, business hubs, universities, military bases**  
- Furnish simply but comfortably  
- Set competitive pricing (occupancy > perfection)  
- Automate cleaning, entry, and communication  
- Build relationships with nearby HR departments and hospitals  

If your market has jobs + mobility, mid-term rentals remain one of the strongest plays this year.

**Cash flow first. Appreciation second.**
